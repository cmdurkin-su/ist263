Welcome to Upstate Dawgs!

Welcome to Upstate Dawgs!! We are your one stop destination for finding the perfect canine. We have created a web browser that enables future owners to find the right dog for them before purchasing. Our team has compiled a large list of some of the most popular breeds. Breaking down their tendencies, looks, price ranges, and habits for users to pick and choose specifically what they are looking for.

Our Mission

Our mission at Upstate Dawgs is to provide comprehensive and accurate information about all dog breeds to help educate and empower dog lovers worldwide. We are dedicated to promoting responsible dog ownership and supporting the welfare of dogs by providing an extensive collection of resources on breeds, health, training, and nutrition. Our goal is to foster a community of dog enthusiasts who share a passion for learning and improving the lives of dogs through knowledge and understanding. Whether you're a first-time dog owner or a seasoned expert, our website is the go-to destination for all your canine-related needs.With our extensive list of the most popular breeds in Upstate New York, we are sure that you will have a better grasp on what type of dog is best for you.
